Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make an appointment using the site??

In order to make an appointment simply click on the "Appointment booking" button under "Online services" on the navigation bar at the top of the page. then choose the appointment you would like to book from the appointments available.

How can I send a message to a doctor via the site?

Simply click on the "Messaging system" button on the navigation bar at the top of the page.click on "New request and fill the needed areas. To send the message click on submit.

How do I change the password of my account?

In order to change the password of the account you can click on "Change password" button under "Profile" button or get into the account's Profile and click there on "Change password" button in the right.

How can I watch my medical file content?

Simply click on the "Medical File" button on the navigation bar at the top of the page.

What can I do in a forum?

In a forum, patients can watch existing forums and ask questions related to the forum subject, doctors and admins can open new forums and answer questions.
