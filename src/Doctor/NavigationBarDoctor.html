About the program:

A prototype version for a health care company in order to connect between the admin of the company, the doctors

and the patients in the clinic through the program.

The system includes different models such as:

Patient could, book an appointment using the program, watch his medical information (Appointment history, appointment summary,

future appointments, prescriptions and test results) and appointments schedule, search for doctors and view their information, watch

forums opened in the system, write a review about a certain doctor in the clinic, message a doctor through a messaging system, rate

a doctor's treatment after an appointment, have a personal profile and get help if needed.

A doctor could, use a messaging system in order to message his patience or other doctors, have a meeting schedule, search for patients in

order to update a new appointment summary, prescriptions or test results in their account, create new forums or answer questions in existing

forums, have a personal profile and get help in the site if needed.

An admin could, use a messaging system in order to message his patience or other doctors, have a meeting schedule, search for patients in order

to update a new appointment summary, prescriptions or test results in their account, create new forums, answer questions in existing forums

and approve new forums created by doctors in the clinic, have a personal profile, answer questions doctors and patients sent in order to get help

and get help in the site if needed.
